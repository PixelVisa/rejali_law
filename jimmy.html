JIMMY NGUYEN


Jimmy Nguyen is a San Diego native and an esteemed law clerk for the firm. Mr. Nguyen received his Juris Doctor degree from Thomas Jefferson School of Law and is pending admission to the California State Bar. As a law student, Mr. Nguyen clerked for the Law Offices of Patenaude & Felix doing civil litigation cases and the County of San Diego Public Defender’s Offices. While at the Public Defender's Offices, Mr. Nguyen worked on multitudes of cases ranging from adult misdemeanor, adult felony, and juvenile delinquency. Mr. Nguyen also did a summer abroad in Bologna, Italy, where he studied International Arbitration and Comparative Law.

Mr. Nguyen is deeply involved with his community. He is a part of and affiliates with grassroots and non-profit organizations in San Diego such as: Asian Pacific American Coalition (APAC) of San Diego, National Association of Asian American Professionals (NAAAP), and the Asian Business Association (ABA). Mr. Nguyen joined APAC in 2011 to voice and advocate for the Asian Pacific Islander community of San Diego for fair representation to the Redistricting Committee. In July of 2011, APAC and Mr. Nguyen, as part of the founding members' group, successfully won recognition for the first Asian Pacific Islander District of San Diego.

Mr. Nguyen received his Bachelors of Science degree in Legal Studies and Psychology at Grand Valley State University in Michigan. After receiving his degree, Mr. Nguyen relocated back to San Diego and worked as a paralegal for several years prior to law school. As a paralegal, Mr. Nguyen worked for the law firm of Naiman Law Group handling foreclosure and unlawful detainer cases, and Pite Duncan, LLP, a bankruptcy firm.

Mr. Nguyen hopes to make a difference in his community by shaping lives and advocating for those who cannot do so on their own.
