RON MCMINN


Ron McMinn is currently a post bar law clerk at Rejali Law Firm. While attending school in San Diego Ron has interned in multiple areas of the law including corporate, criminal defense, personal injury, and immigration law. This wide range of exposure has helped Ron realize his passion for making a difference in the lives of others.

Ron graduated from San Diego State University in 2008 with a bachelor’s degree in Criminal Justice, and then went on to law school to earn his Juris Doctor at Thomas Jefferson School of Law in 2013. He is currently waiting for his results from the most recent California bar exam administered in July 2014. During his time at Thomas Jefferson he was actively involved in several organizations, including the Immigration Law Society and Criminal Law Society.

Ron has also traveled extensively throughout the world where he studied International law and arbitration at Università di Bologna in Bologna, Italy, and International Business and Environmental law at Zhejiang University in Hangzhou, China. He regularly volunteers at Father Joe’s Village in San Diego feeding the homeless and also works with the Salvation Army Kroc Center.

When not working Ron enjoys spending his free time outdoors, taking advantage of the beautiful San Diego weather with family and friends.
